An experiment.

So much goes on inside. I silence it with addiction. With clinging to things. Buying, masturbating, thinking. All this madness.

Sometimes I want to write. And here's where I will do it.

Why barrabas. No one knows barrabas. The dog comes by sea. It's never there. Yet it is the undercurrent of everything.

That's what I want this to be. A place to hide to tell the stories and lies of my life.
