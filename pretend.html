Pretend

Thu 20 July 2017

I'm pretentious. I like to think of myself as some thinker. Fuck, this blog is prententious. Who do I think I am? Some JD Salinger over here trying to write all casually to make some deep point?

I'm such a poser. I say I do certain things. I say I like things I don't like that much. I say I use things that I barely understand.

And it shows. Everyone knows. Everyone can tell and I still do it. People make fun of me for it and I still do it. People mock me for it and I still do it. Why.

And the end of it all, I say I don't care about what others think. I say I'm better, I'm strong, independent thinking, wise. Because Buddha was an independent thinker, and so am I. But I'm here writing a blog post under some phony quasi-intellectual pen name, about why I care. Why I care so much about what people think of me.

What makes me this way? Is it my childhood where father would call me a "dumb kid" and a "sick kid" for getting a cross multiplication wrong? Or was it because of the high standards he put on me, which I now put on myself, and predictably fail to acheive. Even achievement feels useless. There's always something else.

But many others have more fucked up childhoods, and they look fine. I guess I look fine too. I look great on paper. A happy person, leading a 'successful' life with a 'solid career'. That noise is what gets respect. People don't look at you, they look at your credentials. Everything is numbers, money, fakes, lies.

They probably talk behind me at work. I'm a weird guy. Quiet. Socially inept, or at least they see it that way. If only they'd seen me around my friends; I'm only socially inept among mean people. Because I'm scared of being judged. But I judge so quickly.
